About Us

Established in 1928, the Foxhall Community Citizens Association now serves over 900 households in Washington, DC’s Foxhall Village, Colony Hill, Indian Rock, Georgetown Reservoir and Canal View areas. Volunteers deliver a newsletter to every home in the area prior to each general meeting to keep everyone up to date on issues that affect us. With over a quarter of households belonging to the FCCA, we are known as one of Washington’s most active and effective neighborhood associations. Our mission is to preserve the quality of life and unique architectural character of our community and to promote friendship, neighborly feelings and social contacts while advancing the general interests of residents and homeowners.
